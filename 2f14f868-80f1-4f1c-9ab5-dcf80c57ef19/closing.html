Checklist for closing the restaurant

  1. Check all bookings for the next day and set tables accordingly
  2. All tables should have salt flakes, matching water glasses, wine glasses and promotional drinks
  3. All candles should be removed, changed and put away in Station 3 in Sorrento Room in the left hand side cupboard.
  4. Check that all stations are properly organized.
  5. Check all lights from the fridges are off.
  6. Glasswasher has to be turned off and drained.
  7. Check all bins have been cleaned and have a new bin bag. Donâ€™t leave an open bag with food scraps.
  8. The Green bag with dirty linen has to be taken outside. A new one with no dirty linen or tea towels has to be put in the basket.
  9. Bring all sugar bowls, salt and pepper, trays, water jars inside from the station outside.
  10. Check outside table cloths. If dirty, place them in the green bag in the bar. If they are clean, fold them and leave them on Table 7 to be used for the next day.
  11. Wipe with the blue cloth and window cleaner: Front door, tablets, fridge doors and windows if they have fingerprints or marks.
  12. Take the black mat of the hallway outside and leave it rolled up in a crate.
  13. Take the 3 black mats on the back area, roll them up and leave them in a crate.
  14. Check heaters from both sides have been turned off. Switches are placed in the two switch cupboards at the front.
  15. Check all outside lights are off.
    • Turn off all A/C systems.
    • Capri Room: Remote in switch cupboard.
    • Bar: Remote in the Bar
    • Sorrento Room: One remote in the switch cupboard. The other remote on Station 3 next to the Swans.
  16. Check all tablets and EFTPOS machines are connected and charging.
  17. Check Windows and both doors are properly locked.
  18. Check Gas has been turned off (Horizontal is on, Vertical is off.
  19. Check the cool room door is properly shut.
