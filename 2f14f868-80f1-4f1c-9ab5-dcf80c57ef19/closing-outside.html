Checklist for outside area

  1. To fit all furniture from outside you will need to drag Table 7 all the way down to the other side of the door.
  2. Move aside chairs from table 8, 8A, 9, 9A and 15.
  3. All chairs from outside have to be piled up in 2. Bring them in and place them between table 7 and the window.
  4. Bring the outside station (make sure nothing was left on top) and place it in front of the chairs, between Table 7 and the window.
  5. Bring the round table first and leave it in front of Station 1 in Sorrento Room.
  6. Bring the rest of the tables and place 2 between table 7 and 8. The rest on a line in front of table 8 and 9.
  7. Unlock the trolleys and bring them one by one. Be careful with the timber and table 7 when bringing them in.
  8. Open the switch cupboard. Turn off all lights and heaters. Turn off the Air conditioner/ heater with the remote placed in there.
  9. Lock the door and make sure the window is locked too.
