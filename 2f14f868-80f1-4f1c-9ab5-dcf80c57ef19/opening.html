Checklist for opening the restaurant

Indoor Setup

  1. Turn all inside lights - bottom row in Power Cupboard

  2. Turn on outside lights and heaters if it is cold. Dimmed them all up

  3. HOT DAYS: Keep all doors shut and turn on ALL A/C (Bar, Capri A/c, Sorrento front and back A/C)

  4. Lay out the front mat
  5. Check the bookings of the day on the Booking Tablet. Code 101071
    • Set tables accordingly with the bookings
    • Make sure all of them have clean table cloths, cutlery, glasses wine glasses and salt flakes
    • Put on promotional drinks on each one
  6. Make sure tables and chairs are straight
  7. Align all tables in the couch and make a straight line
  8. Table 7: drag and allocate it straight in order to have enough space between table 8, the water station.
  9. Dust all chairs to get rid of crumbs

Outside Setup

  1. Sweep and try to get rid of rubbish and leaves
  2. Check that the trolleys are straight and under the cover. Check the 3 of them have the locks on.
  3. Allocate tables where the marks on the floor are.
  4. Put a piece of cardboard underneath if they wobble
  5. Wipe all tables down. Put tablecloth and table paper on A6
  6. Wipe the outside station down and put out salt, pepper, sugar bowls and a jar of water.
  7. Make sure outside cloth is rinsed and wet to use it during service

General Checks

  1. Undo the curtain and slide it through the rail. Hook the end of it on the window
  2. Allocate the 3 loaves of bread in the bread basket
  3. Place the black long mat along the hallway
  4. Sweep the black mats on the bathroom area
  5. Top up toilet paper and hand paper in all bathrooms
  6. Check all stations have even side plates, bowls and napkins
  7. Allocate 2 jars of water in each station in ‘Sorrento’ room. Rest of big jars at the front section in ‘Capri room’. Small jars on the corner of the bar bench.
  8. Allocate cheese graters and cheese trays: Capri station, another one in Sorrento room small station next to curtain and third one in Sorrento room back station
  9. Check with Chef if we have everything and if specials are still on
  10. Take ‘the baby’ (mobile tablet) out
  11. Do a test run to see all tablets are running
  12. Turn on the music with and make sure volume is the same on both rooms
