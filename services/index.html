Our Services

Working with ATOM Digital gives our clients access to specialists, technical resources.

It allows our clients to focus on what they do best - running their business - while we own and manage their technical landscape.

Typically we help our clients to increase sales, reduce costs and improve productivity in the following ways.

Business Process Automation

We help to:

  • convert paper processes to online ones
  • automate labour intensive, manual processes
  • reduce data entry errors

System Integration

We help to:

  • make multiple systems talk to each other
  • create data integrity between systems
  • push data to business intelligence and reporting tools

Workflow Automation

We help to:

  • automate multi-step, multiple system workflows
  • automate communication to customers, suppliers and employees
  • apply business rules to data and processes

Sales Automation

We help to:

  • ensure a best-in-class digital presence
  • automate lead nurturing and sales follow ups
  • create digital marketing and remarketing campaigns

Inbound Customer Contact

We help to:

  • route calls to the right person at the right time
  • create self-service virtual assistants for callers
  • reduce unanswered and missed calls

Process Outsourcing

We help to:

  • identify processes that can be outsourced
  • select the right outsourced partners
  • document processes and train partners
